My Story

I grew up around a world of gaming consoles, smartphones and computers, and that environment sparked my curiosity about how technology works. I found myself wanting to understand what happens behind the screen and how different systems connect to create the experiences we use every day. That curiosity eventually became the foundation for my decision to pursue a career in technology, and from that point onward I never looked back.

As I progressed through university and my internship experience, I realised that what excites me most is not only building solutions, but also understanding how technology can support businesses, improve processes and help people make better decisions. This is what naturally drew me towards enterprise systems, business analysis, cloud computing and cybersecurity.

Outside technology, I enjoy keeping up with football, spending time with friends and listening to music. My friends would describe me as bubbly, calm and a good listener, which are qualities that help me build relationships and work effectively within a team.

Whenever I encounter a challenge, I prefer asking the right questions before rushing into a solution. I believe understanding the problem first leads to better outcomes. My long-term goal is to become an ethical, dependable technology professional who contributes not only to business success, but also positively impacts the people around me.
